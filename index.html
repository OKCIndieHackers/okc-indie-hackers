Why we started this group in 90 seconds

Values

We operate by a different set of priorities than other entrepreneur-focused meetups. And we like it that way.

Infinite Encouragement 🎉

Building stuff that matters is hard. Don't stop building each other up. There's no room for negativity.

Act Quickly 🏃🏻‍♂️

Don't wait until tomorrow to knock out a quick win when you can do it today. Choose to act quickly and learn from customers rather than slip into endless debate and "What if" questions.

Embrace Bootstrapping 🥾

Having funding and invested can be great, but we don’t discount the effect of a small team with limited resources accomplishing their goals.

Lend a Hand 👋

Anyone who builds something meaningful gets serious help along the way. Pay it forward whenever possible. We're at our best when we cover each other's weak spots.
